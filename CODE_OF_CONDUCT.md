In the interest of fostering an open, welcoming, and professional environment,  
we as contributors and maintainers of **maatify/security-guard** pledge to make participation in our project and community a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to providing a safe and inclusive space for collaboration, regardless of:

- Age
- Gender identity and expression
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Physical appearance
- Body size
- Race
- Ethnicity
- Religion
- Nationality
- Level of experience

## ‚úÖ Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy and professionalism toward other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Trolling, insulting, or using derogatory language
- Personal or political attacks
- Harassment in public or private
- Publishing others‚Äô private information without explicit permission
- Any form of discrimination, intimidation, or threats

## üõ† Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying expected standards of behavior
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Taking appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior
- Removing or editing comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this policy

Maintainers have both the **right and responsibility** to remove, edit, or reject any contribution that does not align with this Code of Conduct.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or behavior.  
**Consequence:** Private, written warning with guidance.

---

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact:** A serious violation or repeated minor incidents.  
**Consequence:** Temporary suspension from project participation.

---

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact:** Sustained inappropriate behavior.  
**Consequence:** Temporary ban from all project spaces.

---

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact:** Harassment, threats, or repeated offenses.  
**Consequence:** Permanent removal from the project and all related platforms.
